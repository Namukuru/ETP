TrackIt


TrackIt, your student-built budgeting buddy, puts you in control of your finances!

This user-friendly app lets you track your income (allowance, part-time job) and expenses (lunch money, school supplies, clothes shopping sprees!) to gain valuable insights into your spending habits. Analyze your data to see where your money goes, set realistic saving goals for that must-have gadget, and even challenge your friends to healthy spending competitions!

TrackIt is still under development, but stay tuned for upcoming educational resources on budgeting and financial responsibility. Share your feedback and be part of the TrackIt movement to turn your financial goals into reality!
